Monitoring and Querying Slashing Events

Monitoring Slash Contractâ€‹

As best practice, it is advisable to keep monitoring the event log of the slash contract on the BSC scanner at https://bscscan.com/address/0x0000000000000000000000000000000000001001#events. Additionally, you can also check the slash indicator by reading the contract and pay attention to those above 30. Once it exceeds 150, the validator contract will be jailed.
